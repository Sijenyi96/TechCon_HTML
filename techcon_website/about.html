History of TechCon

In 2001 with the aim of bringing together technology enthusiasts,innovators and industry leaders. The first conference was held in San Francisco and attracted over 1,000 attendees.

Mission of TechCon

TechCon aims to empower innovators by providing a platform where they can share ideas, showcase their work and connect with like-minded individual. Our Mission is to foster a collaborative environment that nurtures creativity and innovation.

Past speakers and their contributions

Satya Nadella - CEO of Microsoft

Contributions: Led Microsoft transformation with a focus on cloud computing, artificial intelligence and enterprise software, significantly increasing the company market value and relevance in modern technology

Susan Wojcicki - CEO of YouTube

Contributions: Oversaw the growth and monetization of YouTube, making it one of the mos popular video-sharing platforms in the world, and has been an advocate for content creator support and platform diversity.
